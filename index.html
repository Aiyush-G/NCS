NCS - Raising Money for Ukrainian Refugees

As part of our NCS Social Action project we have chosen to raise money and awareness for displaced Ukrainain Refugees in England: creating posters / flyers & hosting a bake-sale.

Since the war began more than 60,000 people fleeing Ukraine have arrived in the UK, however, the experience of settling into UK life has differed wildly for many Ukrainains.

Whilst there are many positive stories, charities warn of overcrowding, lack of funding & financial support with risk of homelessness for many. By raising money to provide support to refugees we hope to mitigate some of the adverse affects of war.
